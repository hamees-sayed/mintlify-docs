# Exciting News: Introducing Our New Waves API!

We're thrilled to announce the launch of our new Waves API, designed to provide you with even more powerful and flexible text-to-speech capabilities.

## Key Features

- **New Endpoint**: Access our latest speech synthesis technology at `waves-api.smallest.ai/api/v1/lightning/get_speech`
- **Enhanced Voice Selection**: Choose from six new voice options, each with its own unique characteristics
- **Improved Performance**: Experience faster processing and higher quality audio output
